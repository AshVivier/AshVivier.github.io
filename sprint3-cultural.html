Emotional Intelligence

How I understand emotional intellegence is the ability to observe and access your, and other peoples current emotional state, and why those emotionas are there.

Being able to focus on the now and correctly assess the situation, and act accordingly in, calm resfectful manner is a particularly useful skill to have, both for our personal relationships, and for our professional relationships.

The diffenence between EQ and IQ

emotional intelligence is the degree to which a person is emotionally in touch with themselves and others and their ability to manage these emotions.
Iq is ones academic/logic comprehesion, such as literacy, science and math, and reasoning.
