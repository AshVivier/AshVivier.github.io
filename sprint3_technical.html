The difference between HTML and CSS

HTML (Hypertext Markup Language) is used to create the actual content of the page, such as written text, and CSS (Cascade Styling Sheets) is responsible for the design or style of the website, including the layout, color, and font styles.

It's like your body is the HTML (which funnily enough also has a head, body, and feet). You can cut your hair, shave, clip your nails etc. Then the cloths that you wear and the jewelry you accsessorize with are the CSS ("style").
